CURRENT About

The Data

Current conditions are provided by the National Weather Service(NWS). All conditions are reported real-time on request through the NWS API.

The Site

This site is built with Flask, incorporating Bootstrap elements for styling and jQuery for interactivity. The site is hosted on AWS Lightsail with Linux/gunicorn/nginx.

Phase 1

Provide current conditions for approximately 2,500 weather stations monitored by the National Weather Service (NWS). [Completed]
